Embedded inside Articulate Rise. Learners test prompts live using the Anthropic API without leaving the course. Two modes: free exploration and PM Agent mode with a locked system prompt.

AI Prompt Sandbox
Test your prompts and see how the AI responds
One-time setup: Paste your Anthropic API key to use this sandbox. Your key stays in this browser session only.
Free mode: Write any prompt. Experiment with the PM Prompting Framework: Role, Context, Task, Constraints, Output format.
PM Agent mode: The system prompt is pre-loaded and locked. Write your product request and experience a configured AI agent.
System prompt (locked)
You are a Senior Product Manager AI Agent. Your role is to help builders think through product decisions clearly and systematically. When asked about a feature or product idea, ask clarifying questions about the target user, the problem being solved, and what success looks like. Then provide structured product thinking.
Component 2
Prompt Comparison

Learners send a weak and a strong prompt side by side. Seeing the difference in AI output quality makes the lesson land better than any explanation.

⚖️
Prompt Comparison
Send both prompts and compare the AI responses side by side
One-time setup: Paste your Anthropic API key.
Weak prompt
Write something about climate change for my report.
Strong prompt
You are an environmental journalist writing for a non-technical audience. Summarise the top 3 causes of climate change in exactly 150 words. Use clear, jargon-free language and end with one specific action individuals can take.
Reflection
What specific differences do you notice in the two responses? Consider: length, specificity, tone, and how closely each response matched what you actually needed. What elements of the strong prompt caused those differences?

Course Become an AI Product Builder: Build and Launch with Agent Workflows Module 1 · Lesson 1.1
Learning Objective
Identify the shift in where human effort goes in AI-era product building, and explain why product thinking is now the primary skill.
🎬 Scenario — Which Builder Has the Right Mindset?
The Situation
Priya has an idea for a tool that automatically summarises customer feedback from emails and sends a weekly digest to her team. She is not a developer. She opens Cursor and types: "Build me an app." Her colleague Marcus has the same idea. He spends 30 minutes writing down exactly who the user is, what the input is, what the output should look like, and what "good" means. Then he opens Cursor.
Your Task
Who will get a better result — and why? What does this tell you about where the real work of being an AI product builder happens?
💡 Hint: The AI builder's advantage is not speed of typing — it is quality of thinking.
❓ Knowledge Check
Which of the following best describes what has changed most significantly for builders in the AI era?
A) Writing code has become faster and easier
B) The bottleneck has shifted from execution to thinking and product judgment
C) You no longer need to understand technology to build digital products
D) AI has replaced the need for product managers
Rise block: Scenario (custom HTML)
Rise block: Knowledge Check with full answer explanations
Standard: Every wrong answer gets a redirect, every correct answer gets the reasoning
